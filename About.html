OUR FACTORY

Woven clothing labels are typically 100% polyester, high- quality, industry-standard labels, and are a renowned choice for versatility and durability, also an ideal choice for clothing and other items to put through the wash as polyester threads can withstand numerous wash cycle keeping imminent fairness and softness throughout the lifetime.

These labels are widely using on,
  • Clothing
  • Bags
  • Shoes
  • Caps
  • Uniforms
  • Furniture’s

    • MATERIALS

    Woven labels are preferred by everyone from high-end designers to small manufacturers. In our factory the polyester threads are woven together on a jacquard loom to present your desired designs and messaging. The resulting label will last for a life time of any garment and will be visually appealing as well. It is the type of weave, and the thickness of the threads that determine the look and feel. Woven labels are generally made with a damask weave or a taffeta weave.
